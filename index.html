Our story

Little kitchen, big memories.

Inspired by Nyonya heritage and Malaysian family tables, Little Nyonya Kitchen is all about slow‑cooked flavours, shared plates, and the feeling of being looked after.

In Malaysian homes, the kitchen is where everything happens—stories, laughter, and the kind of food that makes you stay a little longer at the table. Little Nyonya Kitchen brings that feeling into a cosy dining room, with recipes that blend Chinese, Malay and Nyonya influences.

From fragrant coconut rice and rich rendang to wok‑fried noodles and sambal‑kissed seafood, each dish is cooked with care, layered with spice, and served with the warmth of true hospitality.

Whether you’re discovering Malaysian flavours for the first time or coming in for a taste of home, this is a place to slow down, share plates, and enjoy food that’s made to be remembered.

Cosy, welcoming atmosphere Soft lighting, warm colours and friendly service make the space feel like a living room, not a restaurant.
